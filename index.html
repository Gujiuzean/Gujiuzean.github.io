Efficient Text-to-Audio Generation Integrated with Fine-Grained Music Style Modulation

Anonymous submission

Overview

The illustration of our proposed framework. (a) depicts the AuDilya model, which leverages two-way Mamba andcross-attention to construct an efficient TTA architecture. In (b), we introduce a music style knob plug-in by incorporating a low-rank decomposition matrix into each cross-attention laver within AuDiMa, During training, a low-rank adapter is fne.tuned using paired sets of text related to musical styles. During inference, users can input text content and adjust a knob tocontrol the intensity of the music style.

Conclusion

This paper introduces AuDiMa, a streamlined TTA back-bone equipped with a fine-grained music style control module, advancing audio/music generation from a creative artistic perspective. The limitation of this study is its omission of long music generation and multi-style fusion. We will address these aspects in the future to enhance the model's capabilities.
